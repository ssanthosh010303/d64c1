How to Play?

Wordle is a popular word puzzle game where the objective is to guess a hidden five-letter word within six attempts. Here's how to play:

To start, enter any five-letter word as your first guess. After submitting your guess, the game provides feedback with colored tiles: green tiles indicate correct letters in the correct positions, yellow tiles indicate correct letters in different positions, and gray tiles indicate incorrect letters. Use this feedback to refine your subsequent guesses and deduce the correct word.

Strategize by beginning with a common five-letter word to gauge initial feedback. Adjust your guesses based on the colored tiles to eliminate possibilities and hone in on the target word efficiently. The game ends successfully if you guess the correct word within five attempts. If you exhaust all attempts without guessing correctly, you lose the game.

Wordle typically updates with a new puzzle each day, offering players a fresh challenge. It's a game that tests your vocabulary and deduction skills in a straightforward and engaging format.
